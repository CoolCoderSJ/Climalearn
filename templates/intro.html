Climalearn

Aiming to educate the general population on the effects of climate change.

Each year, the amount of CO2 emitted into the atmosphere increases, and keeps hurting the earth. It has become a global concern, leading the UN to make it one of their 17 Sustainability Development Goals. Goal 13 task 3 (13.3) is to educate the general public about the effects of CO2 on the environment. This website aims to do just that. With Climalearn, you can see what changes NASA predicts to the place you live in, and find out how you can help slow this down for a better future.
