Climate change is changing the Earth, and it's happening fast.

The following is an overview of what is predicted to happen over the next few years because of climate change.

  1. Air temperatures are rising, and on average, the high and low temperature for each day will be hotter.
  2. Sea levels are rising, which means more frequent floods and more damaging natural disasters.
  3. The oceans are heating up, which causes sea levels to rise and kills many marine animals.
  4. The amount of droughts in the US is rising each year, which means damaged crops and moving millions of people.

Don't turn a blind eye on climate change. Help out by doing simple things and reduce the amount of carbon emissions that go into the air each year.
