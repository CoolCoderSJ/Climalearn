Public Transport

Transport accounts for a big part of total greenhouse gas emissions, up to 25% according to the UNEP. By using public transport, there are less vehicles on the road, helping lower emissions.
Find Public Transport Near You

Convert to Solar Energy

Choosing a renewable energy source helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions while also saving money. The US Department of Energy has also shown that power sources for electric vehicles like Natural Gas, Coal, and Nuclear power cause the most emissions, while power sources like Hydro power, Solar power, and power from biomass cause the least emissions. (View Statistics)

Shop at Local Markets

Local markets tend to use sustainable agriculture, which uses to 56% less energy and creates 64% fewer emissions.
Find Local Markets Near You

Plant Trees

Many forests are taken down each year due to deforestation and forest fires. By planting trees, you can help regrow these forests.
